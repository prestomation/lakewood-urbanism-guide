            Glossary

            Glossary#

            Plain-language definitions for urban planning terms that come up in Lakewood City Council meetings, Planning Commission hearings, and community discussions.

            Terms are organized by category:

            • Housing & Zoning — ADU, conditional use, variances, residential districts, historic preservation
            • Transportation — Complete streets, mode share, LOS, active transportation, transit
            • Land Use — Community Vision, comprehensive planning, zoning code, NEPA/SEPA
            • Funding & Policy — TIF, CDBG, CRA, impact fees, tax abatement
