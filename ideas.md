# Content Ideas

Prioritized suggestions for new guides, glossary terms, timeline entries, and blog posts.

## Guides

- **Commercial Corridors** — Detroit Avenue and Madison Avenue development history, current businesses, revitalization programs, storefront renovation, Startup Lakewood
- **Parks and Green Space** — Lakewood Park, neighborhood parks, Lake Erie waterfront access, recreation programming
- **Historic Preservation** — Birdtown Historic District, Heritage Advisory Board, Architectural Board of Review, preservation incentives
- **Schools and Walkability** — Lakewood City Schools' no-bus-service model, school siting, Safe Routes to School, walking school buses
- **Climate Resilience** — Lake Erie shoreline, stormwater management, urban tree canopy, resiliency planning
- **Micro-mobility** — E-scooters, bike share potential, first/last mile solutions

## Timeline Events

- 1819: Rockport Township established
- 1903: Lakewood incorporated as a village
- 1930s: Peak population era
- 1950s-60s: Streetcar removal and transition to buses
- 2004: LCPI founded (predecessor to LakewoodAlive)
- 2005: National Main Street designation
- 2016: Lakewood Hospital closure
- 2019: Community Vision data update
