# Glossary

Plain-language definitions for urban planning terms that come up in Lakewood City Council meetings, Planning Commission hearings, and community discussions.

Terms are organized by category:

- [Housing & Zoning]({{< relref "housing-zoning" >}}) — ADU, conditional use, variances, residential districts, historic preservation
- [Transportation]({{< relref "transportation" >}}) — Complete streets, mode share, LOS, active transportation, transit
- [Land Use]({{< relref "land-use" >}}) — Community Vision, comprehensive planning, zoning code, NEPA/SEPA
- [Funding & Policy]({{< relref "funding-policy" >}}) — TIF, CDBG, CRA, impact fees, tax abatement
