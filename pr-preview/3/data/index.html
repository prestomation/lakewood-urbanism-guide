            Data Sources#

            Public data sources, dashboards, and maps useful for understanding development, transportation, and housing in Lakewood.

            City of Lakewood#

            Lakewood Zoning Map#

            Interactive map showing zoning districts across the city.

            Access: Lakewood Zoning Map

            Community Vision Website#

            The city’s planning framework with data on commercial development, housing, mobility, and other focus areas.

            Access: Lakewood Community Vision

            Planning Commission Agendas & Applications#

            Upcoming Planning Commission hearings and development applications submitted to the city.

            Access: Citizenserve Portal

            Board of Zoning Appeals Agendas#

            Upcoming BZA hearings for variance requests and zoning appeals.

            Access: BZA Agendas

            Cuyahoga County#

            Cuyahoga County GIS#

            Geographic information system with property data, parcel boundaries, and land use information for all of Cuyahoga County.

            Access: Cuyahoga County GIS

            Cuyahoga County Fiscal Officer - Property Search#

            Property tax records, valuations, and ownership information.

            Access: Cuyahoga County Property Search

            Regional & State#

            RTA System Map and Schedules#

            Route maps, schedules, and real-time arrival information for all Greater Cleveland RTA bus and rail services.

            Access: RideRTA Routes

            Walk Score - Lakewood#

            Walkability, transit, and bike scores for Lakewood neighborhoods and addresses.

            Access: Walk Score - Ohio

            Ohio Codified Ordinances - Lakewood#

            Full text of Lakewood’s codified ordinances including the Planning and Zoning Code.

            Access: Lakewood Codified Ordinances

            US Census - Lakewood, Ohio#

            Demographic, housing, and economic data from the American Community Survey and decennial census.

            Access: Census QuickFacts - Lakewood
